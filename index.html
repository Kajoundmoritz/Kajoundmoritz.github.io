Arriving by Plane

Of the two airports in Paris, we recommend using Charles De Gaulle from where it is a 60-90 minute drive to the venue. We are thinking about providing a bus on Friday from Charles De Gaulle - please reach out if you are interested!

Arriving by Train

The closest major train station is Amiens. From there, a taxi or rental car can take you to the château in about 30 minutes.

Arriving by Car

Driving from Paris is straightforward via the A16 and D933. Parking is available on-site. We are happy to connect you with others for ridesharing options, so please reach out to us!

Accommodations

Château De Vauchelles

The château itself offers 18 rooms and 3 apartments for 60 guests. We would like to prioritize families with children, but we don't anticipate that this will exhaust all rooms. The booking of the rooms has to go through us, so if you are interested in staying at the château, please reach out to us.

Hotel Website

Hôtel Jean De Bruges

The hotel Jean De Bruges, located at the gateway to the Baie de Somme, has 9 rooms for 16 guests. All rooms have been reserved for the wedding. Bed and breakfast is included. The hotel is approximately 14 minutes by car from the venue.

Hotel Website

Relais Du Beffroi

The hotel Le Relais du Beffroi, located in Saint-Riquier (Somme), offers a spa and private parking. There 8 rooms for 16 guests and 3 children. All rooms have been reserved for the wedding. The hotel is approximately 14 minutes by car from the venue.
